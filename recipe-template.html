45 mins
 

Vegan
 

Easy
 

30 waffles
Ingredients
500g sugar
500g margarine, room temperature
10 egg-substitute
2 pck. vanilla sugar
1 shot rum
1kg flour
1l oat milk
1 pck. baking soda

Method

Mix the egg substitute with the sugar and margarine until a fluffy dough forms.

Add vanilla sugar and a good dash of real rum.

Add 1kg flour and the baking powder and stir, this will make the dough a little sticky.

Gradually stir in the oat milk and mix until the dough has a smooth and soft consistency.

Bake the waffles in batches in a hot waffle iron and serve hot.
